Institutional Statement

Formal declaration of institutional positioning, independence, and governance orientation of Sali Labs.

Independent Research Initiative

Sali Labs operates as an independent research initiative dedicated to structural engineering intelligence, infrastructure resilience modeling, and analytical framework development.

The initiative is not affiliated with governmental, academic, or corporate institutions unless such affiliation is formally declared within structured agreements.

Institutional Neutrality

Sali Labs maintains analytical neutrality and methodological independence in all research, advisory, and collaborative engagements.

Research directions are guided by engineering integrity, systems-level evaluation, and long-horizon infrastructure logic.

Governance Alignment

All activities operate under structured governance principles, including transparency of assumptions, reproducibility standards, and ethical engineering practice.

Public Communication Policy

Public representations of Sali Labs reflect its status as an independent research platform. No partnership, endorsement, or institutional affiliation should be inferred unless explicitly documented.

Intellectual Positioning

Sali Labs contributes to the advancement of structural systems architecture, infrastructure intelligence, and resilience-based engineering methodologies through structured analytical development and research dissemination.
