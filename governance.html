Research Governance

Institutional principles guiding analytical integrity, methodological rigor, and independent structural engineering research activities.

Governance Philosophy

Sali Labs operates under a structured governance model designed to ensure analytical independence, methodological transparency, and institutional neutrality.

All research activities are conducted in accordance with established engineering ethics, reproducibility standards, and structured validation protocols.

Research Integrity Standards

Methodological Transparency

Clear documentation of analytical assumptions, modeling structures, and validation procedures.

Analytical Independence

Independent research direction and topic selection, unless formal institutional collaboration is explicitly established.

Reproducibility Framework

Structured computational workflows designed to enable validation and peer-review assessment.

Methodological Architecture

Research initiatives follow a structured cycle:

  • Problem formulation and hypothesis development
  • Computational model construction
  • Validation and uncertainty quantification
  • Sensitivity analysis and system evaluation
  • Strategic infrastructure applicability assessment

Emphasis is placed on analytical rigor, transparency of assumptions, and institutional accountability.

Institutional Position

Sali Labs operates as an independent research initiative and is not affiliated with governmental, academic, or corporate institutions unless formally declared.
