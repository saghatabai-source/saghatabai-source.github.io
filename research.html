Research Framework

Applied structural intelligence, infrastructure resilience modeling, and long-horizon system optimization aligned with national-scale transformation agendas.

Research Direction

Sali Labs focuses on advanced structural engineering systems, risk-based infrastructure modeling, and integrated strategic design. The research approach combines computational simulation, resilience theory, and macro-scale infrastructure planning.



Core Research Areas

Seismic Resilience Modeling

Nonlinear structural analysis, collapse mechanism studies, and performance-based design under extreme loading conditions.

Infrastructure Systems Integration

Multi-layer infrastructure coordination including transport, utilities, energy networks, and structural frameworks.

Strategic Risk Forecasting

Long-term probabilistic risk assessment, scenario-based infrastructure planning, and adaptive resilience architecture.



Methodological Approach

The research methodology integrates finite element modeling, system dynamics analysis, and macro-scale strategic evaluation. Emphasis is placed on bridging academic rigor with institutional-level decision frameworks.



Research Agenda (2026–2031)

The structured five-year research roadmap outlines the strategic development of AI-assisted structural systems, infrastructure resilience modeling, and long-horizon megaproject engineering frameworks.
